Omurice (Cosmos Special)

Finished Product




Description

  • Experience Required: Intermediate
  • This is just a classic omurice recipe. The hardest part as usual, is making the fluffy omlette.
  • The twist that makes this dish special is the smiley face drawn with ketchup.

Ingredients

  • 2-4 eggs
  • 100-200 g of rice (not jasmine)
  • some vegetables (add or remove as desired):
    • garlic
    • onions
    • carrots
    • green peas
    • potatoes
  • ketchup

Steps

  1. Follow a YouTube tutorial on omurice (preferrably by a japanese guy who looks like he's being doing this recipe for decades)
  2. Add the smiley face as shown on the image. (Be extremely careful, very difficult to undo mistake!)
